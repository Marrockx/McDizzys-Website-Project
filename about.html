MCDIZZYS

MCDIZZYS is an organisation conceived with the aim of impacting knowledge of desserts to people by Mariam Adekola.This will increase the awareness of desserts by giving ideas of what type of desserts to have after your meal, how to incorporate desserts into your meal and its benefits while ensuring a healthy diet. I believe the food we take into our body system is very important for our wellbeing. I chose to specialise on desserts specifically because it contributes a whole lot to how the skin behaves i.e. how the skin ages, and can aid in maintaining a youthful look.
