Generally, Creamy custards and puddings usually include a thickened dairy base. The type of thickener(which could be starch, egg proteins, etc.)used determines whether it is a custard or a pudding.

Custards

Custard is a great snack or dessert for the family. Custards are creamy, cooked deserts, thickened with eggs. Not only does custard taste great, but it’s packed with the goodness of milk including calcium and protein. Traditional custard mixture is thickened by either starch or egg proteins. Flan is an open, rimmed, pastry or sponge base, containing a sweet or savoury filling. Examples of flan are the custard tart, and the African melktert. Puddings

Puddings are commercially produced milk-based desserts with various flavours that can be enjoyed alone or mixed with different ingredients. Pudding contains a wide spectrum of carbohydrates. Milk proteins make pudding a source of valuable proteins. Skimmed milk powder is sometimes added to puddings to increase their protein content. Puddings are thickened with starches. Variations on puddings include vegan puddings made from silken tofu and instant pudding.

Two common types of puddings are rice and tapioca. Some examples of pudding include: chocolate cornstarch pudding, banana pudding, chocolate cream pudding, vanilla pudding, butterscotch pudding, etc.

Frozen desserts include ice cream, gelato, sorbet and water ices, frozen yoghurt, sherbets, frozen custard or french ice cream, frozen confeection, granita, semifreddo, ice cream sandwich, etc. Nothing beats the heat on a hot summer day than a cooling ice cream. Ice cream consists of cream slowly stirred in a churn to freeze it to a creamy consistency. Gelato uses a milk base instead of cream and has less air mixed into it than ice cream. Frozen custard uses a cooked base of egg yolks. For a dairy-free dessert, try a sorbet, which is made from churned fruit purees. Frozen yoghurt uses yoghurt rather than the heavier cream base of ice cream, which also makes it a lower calorie treat.

Cakes have their origins in sweet breads. Cakes are not just for birthday parties. Examples of cakes include angel food cake made with egg whites, sponge cake, flourless cakes (like the sacher torte, German chocolate cake, pavolva cake made with a meringue base), fruitcakes (like banana cake, carrot cake, strawberry cake), pound cake, foam cake, cheese cake, chiffon cake, red velvet cake, genoise, chocolate sponge cake, cassata, etc. The Petits fours, cupcakes and snack cakes are single-serve sizes of larger cakes.

Cookies originated as small cakes. They were spoonfuls of cake batter cooked to test the oven temperature before ovens had thermometers. Cookies vary in their size, shape and texture, but many cookies share variations of basic ingredients of sweetener, eggs, butter or shortening and flour. Varieties include chocolate chip cookies, soft sugar cookies, crunchy gingersnaps, tender shortbread, chewy oatmeal cookies, crispy meringues and cakelike bar cookies.

A double crust indicates that this dessert is a type of pie instead of a tart. The simplest form of pie involves a crust with a filling. The crust can be on the top and bottom of the filling or just on the bottom, made from pastry or graham cracker crumbs. Pie fillings include custards, puddings, nuts and fruits. Chocolate cream pie, lemon meringue pie, shoo-fly pie, pecan pie, apply pie and tarts are pie and tarty examples. Tarts use a baking pan with straight sides and do not have a top crust.

Fudge is a rich, dense chocolate candy with tiny sugar crystals. Chocolates and candy involve the crystallization of sugar for their creation. The size of the sugar crystals determines the texture of the candy. Rock candy has large sugar crystals and a crunchy texture, but fudge contains small sugar crystals, giving it a smooth taste. Candy and chocolate desserts include fudge, caramel, lollipops, taffy, marshmallows, fondant, pralines and cotton candy.

The same flaky pastry used to make pie crusts is applied to other sweet pastry desserts such as cream puffs, baklava, eclairs, Danish pastries, palmiers, profiteroles, bread etc.. Some desserts do not fit nicely into any one category. Cheesecake is one example. Though it is called cake, it more closely resembles a tart, but the filling is basically a custard. Desserts featuring simply prepared fruits also do not fit well into other categories. Flaming bananas foster and cherries jubilee are fruity desserts with a syrup and alcohol sauce. This sauce is lit at the table before eating as a flashy presentation to end the meal.
